Cloudiness

There appears to be a greater reporting of the extremities of cloudiness across the latitudes (i.e. more locations reported cloudiness between 0-10% and 80-100% than those reporting 10-80%). Cloudiness appears to be more evenly distributed in the southern hemisphere than the northern hemisphere.
